About NYC Open Data

NYC Open Data provides public access to datasets collected by city agencies.

About This Dataset

This project uses the 2018 Central Park Squirrel Census dataset. Volunteers recorded squirrel fur color, age, location, shift, and behavior throughout Central Park.

Why This Data Matters

The census helps researchers understand urban wildlife and squirrel behavior.
